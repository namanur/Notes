## [1] Concept Explanation
Certification pathways represent the formal validation of an **Agent** Architect's ability to design, deploy, and govern autonomous systems. In the context of this manual, professional certification serves as the external "Grill Me" process for your career. We map the technical skills acquired in previous layers (L0–L3) to professional standards, specifically focusing on three pillars:

1.  **CCA-F (Claude Certified Architect – Foundations):** Validates core **Reasoning** orchestration, **Context Window** management, and **DDD** application.
2.  **MCP Protocol Cert:** Validates the ability to build standardized interfaces (**Tools** and **Resources**) that allow agents to interact with legacy systems (e.g., accounting software).
3.  **Google Cloud Skill Boost:** Provides the production-grade **Harness** (Vertex AI, Cloud Run) required for high-availability **Action** execution.

Mapping the manual to CCA-F domains:
- **Foundations:** L0 (Harness, Perception).
- **Design:** L1 (DDD, TDD, MCP).
- **Security & Governance:** L2 & L3 (Role Isolation, PEP/PDP, Policy Enforcement).
- **Operations:** L3 & L4 (Swarms, Orchestration, Leveraging B2B services).
