# Phase 5: Autonomous Mastery — System Synthesis & Review (Day 40)

## 🧠 Mental Model: The Infinite Loop (Continuous Learning)
The completion of 40 days is not an "end"; it is the transition from **Manual Execution** to **System Orchestration**.

### The Synthesis Pillars
1.  **Architecture Review:** Identifying the "Bottleneck" (e.g., latency in MCP bridges or context window saturation).
2.  **Safety & Alignment:** Reviewing the agent's behavior for "Plan-Drift" (when the agent deviates from the goal).
3.  **The Meta-Agent:** Creating an agent whose only job is to manage, update, and optimize other agents.
4.  **Hardware/Software Symbiosis:** Ensuring your local environment (Fedora/Ubuntu) is optimized for high-volume agentic traffic.

**Brutal Truth:** A system that doesn't evolve is legacy the moment it's finished. Your job is now to build the **"Evolution Engine."**
